Nitrux 6.1.0 includes a major refresh of the Maui App suite, a new application launcher, and enhanced security with YubiKey support. This update also provides the latest hardware compatibility and important bug fixes.

This update also resolves issues with high-DPI monitor scaling, the Calamares installer auto-login toggle, and TTY shell rendering.
